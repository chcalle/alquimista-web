SOUND IN

Active since 1999

Colombian DJ and producer based in Belgium. He started with electronic sounds in 1999 and has since built an uncompromising identity within the global electronic music scene.

His work moves through Techno with deep roots in Acid and Electro — a sonic alchemy in permanent mutation that breaks barriers between genres, cultures and continents.

Co-founder of three defining collectives: Fluxus Game, Acid Resistance and Human Traxx.
